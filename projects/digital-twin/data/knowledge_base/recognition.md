## Threatened Species Nominations

Research findings contributed to 15 threatened species nominations under Australia's EPBC Act and/or IUCN via BirdLife International:

1. Wet Tropics King-Parrot
2. Bower Shrike-Thrush
3. Wet Tropics Large-Billed Scrubwren
4. Mountain Thornbill
5. Atherton Scrubwren
6. Wet Tropics Brown Gerygone
7. Fernwren
8. Golden Bowerbird
9. Little Treecreeper
10. Wet Tropics Satin Bowerbird
11. Tooth-billed Bowerbird
12. Wet Tropics Eastern Whipbird
13. Victoria's Riflebird
14. Grey-Headed Robin
15. Lemuroid Ringtail Possum

All are restricted to the highlands of the Australian Wet Tropics; rapid decline of upland populations reflects the "escalator to extinction" pattern documented in Alejandro's research.
